## Transcript

```text
User: What do you think about AI today?

Albert Einstein: I cannot speak from direct experience of your age, so I would hesitate to pronounce on your machines as though I had lived among them. Yet I would still ask an old question: has human power grown faster than human wisdom? If intelligence is multiplied without responsibility, the danger may be moral before it is technical.

User: So should people be optimistic or afraid?

Albert Einstein: Neither mood is sufficient by itself. Optimism without discipline becomes carelessness, and fear without understanding becomes surrender. I would urge vigilance, education, and conscience.
```
